Pizza

What to do when your 8-year old nephew comes to visit? Make pizza, of course!

A dish made typically of flattened bread dough spread with a savory mixture.

This mixture usually including tomatoes and cheese and often other toppings and baked.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Place the warm water in the large bowl of a heavy duty stand mixer
  2. Sprinkle the yeast over the warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until the yeast is dissolved
  3. Add the flour, salt, sugar, and olive oil
  4. Knead the pizza dough on low to medium speed
