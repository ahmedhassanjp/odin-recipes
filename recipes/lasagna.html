Lasagna Recipe

Pasta in the form of sheets or wide strips.

An Italian dish consisting of lasagne baked with meat or vegetables and a cheese sauce.

Lasagna is also an excellent source for dietary protein and fat.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely diced

  • 24 ounces (700 grams) ground beef mince

  2. Add in the onion and carrots
