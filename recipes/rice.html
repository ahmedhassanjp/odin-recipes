Rice

The most important cereal crop in the developing world

The staple food of over half the world's population.

It is generally considered a semi-aquatic annual grass plant.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil optional
  • 1 cup long grain white rice

Steps

  1. Bring the water to boil in a medium saucepan
  2. Add the salt and butter and allow the butter to melt
  3. stir in the rice. Let the water return to a light simmer
  4. When the rice is cooked, turn off the heat and let it sit for another couple of minutes to finish absorbing any liquid
